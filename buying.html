Know more about Diamonds !
If you are concerned about getting true alue for your money, know what you are getting.

Cut

Cut refers to the proportions, polish, and symmetry of a stone: these three factors are important in creating a diamond with the best possible light reflection.

Colour

Colour is the composition of a diamond, which is evaluated based on its degree of colourlessness. Diamonds are graded on a Whiteness scale or absence of colour scale.

Clarity

Clarity, or purity, is a diamond’s ability to let in light and reflect its brilliance. Clarity is the easiest for you to evaluate and the most straightforward to understand.
