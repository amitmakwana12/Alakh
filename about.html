About Us

Alakh Diomond is most respected manufacturers and distributors of Polished Chakri, English Polki Diomond and Double cut Diomond in diamond world. We are straight edged diamond manufacturers and Known for our precious cutting and polishing of diamond for jewellery making.


We are running our business on the principle of mutual respected quality and dedicated our services. Our dedication to quality assures that every stone which we deliver to our client has the full weight of our reputation and integrity behind it.

Our vertically integrated business approach spans from procurement of raw materials to the sales, manufacture, distribution, and after-sales service of diamond. From rough to retail, ATM has successfully established a significant global presence in the diamond world.
